# WP Airline Manager 4

WordPress plugin to keep track of stats for your [Airline Manager 4](https://www.airline4.net/) fleet.

* CPTs

** Route
	** Route Flags Tax

** Fleet Plane
	** Tax: Plane
	** Tax: Fleet Flags

** Flights
	** Income
	** CPT: Route
	** CPT: Fleet Plane

* Taxonomies

** Fleet Flags
	** Speed Boost
	** Fuel Boost
	** CO2 Boost

** Route Flags
	** Optimal
	** High Profit
	** Low Profit
	** Needs Attention
